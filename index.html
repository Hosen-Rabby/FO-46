HOW TO WIN A GAME?

A game consists of three rounds. To win a round, the number you choose must be the closest to the number designated by the roulette. To win all three rounds at once and win the game, your number must match the one on the roulette. This means the game ends and you win the total bet of the game. In case of a tie, the winnings are divided among the winners.
